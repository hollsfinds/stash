Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Stash is a no-download savings tracker by Holls Finds Digital ("we," "us," "our"). The short version of this policy: Stash keeps your information on your own device, and we don't collect it.

What we collect: essentially nothing

Stash has no accounts, no login, and no servers that store your information. Everything you enter, your goals, deposits, nickname, and settings, is saved only in your own device's browser storage. We never see it, and it never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

Stash does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies.

The one exception: messages you send us

If you choose to use the in-app suggestion box or join the "Stash Plus" waitlist, the information you type there (your message, your wishlist, and your email address if you provide one) is sent to us by email through a third-party form service, Web3Forms. We use it only to read your feedback and, if you asked, to reply or add you to the waitlist. We do not sell or share it. Web3Forms' own privacy practices are described on their website.

Your data is in your hands

Because your data lives on your device:

  • You can export a backup file or a CSV at any time from Settings.
  • You can delete everything at any time from Settings, using "Clear all data."
  • Clearing your browser data, deleting the app from your home screen, or switching devices will erase your Stash data. We have no copy and no way to recover it, so please back up regularly.

Hosting

The Stash app is served as static files from GitHub Pages. Like any website host, GitHub's servers may automatically log standard technical request information (such as IP address and browser type) for security and operation. We do not control or use those logs. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Children

Stash is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above always shows the latest version.

Contact

The easiest way to reach us is the suggestion box inside the Stash app: open Settings and look for "Questions or feedback?" Your message comes straight to us. If you bought Stash on Etsy, you can also message us through our Holls Finds shop on Etsy.
